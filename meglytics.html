Customer Segmentation

With mushrooming customer acquisition costs, it has become more important to target promotions and fix pricing, increase profitability effectively.

To deal with all the data they needed and the pressure to increase profitability any retail company can use our proof of concept Meglytics Visualizer to correlate purchase history, profile information and products catalog in figuring out a pattern of customers buying behaviour.

The same product can be provided at different price. Segment different customers, by predicting customer buying behaviour willingness to pay. Explore Price sensitivity on customers willing to pay a higher pay and in effect impact on customers lost ratio to profitability.

Recommendation Engine

Identify the best offers to send to a customer using collaborative filtering. In Retail if a customer buys laptop the customer might be interested in a mouse..

Market Basket Analysis

Identify product affinities with and across segments easily. A customers often purchase shampoo and conditioner together, so putting both items on promotion at the same time would not create a significant increase in revenue.

Churn Prediction

Identify customers with probability to churn and target them with relevant offers to improve retention. The ability to predict that a particular customer is at a high risk of churning, while there is still time to do something about it, represents a huge additional potential revenue source.
