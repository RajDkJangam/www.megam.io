Meglytics


Analyze data and identify patterns, so as to predict "what will happen next".

Customer Segmentation


With mushrooming customer acquisition costs, it has become more important to target promotions and fix pricing, increase profitability effectively. To deal with all the data they needed and the pressure to increase profitability any retail company can use our proof of concept Meglytics Visualizer to correlate purchase history, profile information and products catalog in figuring out a pattern of customers buying behaviour.

The same product can be provided at different price. Segment different customers, by predicting customer buying behaviour willingness to pay. Explore Price sensitivity on customers willing to pay a higher pay and in effect impact on customers lost ratio to profitability.

Features


  • Market Basket Analysis: Identify product affinities with and across segments easily

  • Churn Prediction: Identify customers with probability to churn and target them with relevant offers to improve retention

  • Campaign propensity: Make your campaigns more efficient both in terms of budget and effectiveness

  • Recommendation Engine: Identify the best offers to send to a customer using collaborative filtering
