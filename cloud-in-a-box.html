Hybrid Cloud

Cloud ready on-premise infrastructure in a few clicks. We support hybrid cloud launch on AWS EC2, Google, HP, Profit Bricks, GoGrid, Softlayer and OpenNebula/OpenStack.

Disaster recovery

Having your stack up after an apocalypse? No problem, we provide an on-premise HA configured private cloud in a few clicks using a WebUI. Available for free and built on open source.

Runs on any hardware

We are hardware agnostic, any hardware in your datacenter can be cloud ready in minutes. Fujitsu's RX-line of servers is recommended. We do not get a discount for this recommendation, we genuinely love the quality of their gear.

Replicated Storage

Alternative to SAN in a cost effective way. We use open source technology "Ceph" to deliver replicated storage on commodity hardware. Rock solid fault tolerant storage.
