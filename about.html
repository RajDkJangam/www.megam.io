About Us

Megam is an end-to-end open source cloud management platform offered as a bundled offering with IaaS and can run apps, services, micro services (Docker), Analytics on hybrid cloud.

Megam was founded in March 2013. We are a fun filled, passionate team of 4 and bunch of open source contributors. We are located in Chennai, India. Megam in tamil dialect stands for Cloud.
